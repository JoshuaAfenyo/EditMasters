Editing & Proofreading Tips

Tip #1: Read Aloud

Reading your text aloud can help catch awkward phrasing and errors you might miss when reading silently.

Tip #2: Take Breaks

Give yourself time between writing and editing to see your work with fresh eyes.

Tip #3: Focus on One Issue at a Time

First check for grammar and spelling, then focus on style and flow.
